Story of Poseidon Part 1

Poseidon is the god of the sea and has two brothers, Zeus and Hades. Zeus is the god of earth and Hades is god of underworld.

Poseidon was a jealous man and he decided he wanted to be appreciated by the land people.

He knew that Athens was trying to decide which god they wanted to build their temple for. Can you guess what he did next?
